    Why Inertia Works

    Each feature mapped to peer-reviewed research

    Procrastination isn't a character flaw — it's a predictable response to specific conditions your brain finds aversive. Vague goals, distant rewards, and overwhelming tasks all trigger avoidance.

    Inertia is built around findings from behavioural psychology, cognitive science, and habit research — not gamification tricks. Every field in the task flow exists because a peer-reviewed study shows it meaningfully increases follow-through.
